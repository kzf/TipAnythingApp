F.A.Q.

Owners are the users who manage the tipping sheet. They can change questions, options and are responsible for freezing questions and for entering the correct result once it is known. Make sure you trust the owners of your league to be honest and accurate in their management! Freezing a question means that players answers are locked in and they can no longer change them. It is important you do not forget to freeze the questions before the event happens to ensure sneaky users do not come and choose the correct outcome once they know it. You may choose to freeze your questions a few days before the event to make sure.
